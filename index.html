More About Us

Reliable, tamper-proof network.

PensionBlock uses decentralized database and once the information is recorded, it cannot be altered or deleted without consensus from the network participants, making it virtually impossible for pension records to be tampered with.

Secure network.

PensionBlock uses cryptographic algorithms to secure data and prevent unauthorized access preventing fraudulent activity, hacking attempts, and other types of cyber-attacks that could compromise the integrity of pension systems.

Transparent, moderated network

All transactions on PensionBlock are recorded and timestamped, creating an audit trail that can be used to verify the authenticity and accuracy of pension records.
